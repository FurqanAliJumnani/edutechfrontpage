Programs

Our Programs

End-to-end educational solutions covering teacher training, school management, curriculum development, and technology integration.

Early Childhood Education (ECE) Certification Program

A comprehensive certification program designed for Montessori and ECE educators. It focuses on child development, interactive learning environments, and activity-based teaching aligned with modern educational standards. The program includes hands-on training, lesson planning, and assessment strategies to build a strong foundation in early years education.

Teachers' Training & Professional Development Program

This program offers capacity-building workshops and continuous professional development sessions for educators across all levels. Topics include: Innovative and digital teaching methodologies, Classroom management and student engagement, Subject-specific enhancement (Math, Science, English, etc.), STEM and project-based learning.

School Improvement Plan (SIP)

Our flagship program designed to uplift schools through a complete academic and operational transformation. SIP includes: Teacher training and monitoring, E-learning lab setup, Student learning portal access, Creative worksheets, EduTech publications, and more, Multi-campus academic support and school management software.

Student Assessment & Test Generation Program

A systemized program for academic performance measurement through: Customized exam paper generation (monthly, term-wise), Diagnostic, formative, and summative assessments, Progress tracking tools and student performance reports, Support for online and offline test formats.

Single National Curriculum (SNC) Training & Publication

EduTech offers comprehensive teacher orientation and training on the Single National Curriculum (SNC), including: SNC-based content delivery techniques, Integration of moral, civic, and modern learning values, Custom SNC-aligned textbooks, workbooks, and guides developed by EduTech's publication wing.

Sindh Textbook Board Upgradation & Digitalization Program

An exclusive initiative to upgrade and digitize the Sindh Textbook Board content through: Interactive and digital textbook versions, Visual lesson enhancements and video lectures, Digital worksheets, quizzes, and teacher guides, Support for hybrid learning in both English and Urdu mediums.
